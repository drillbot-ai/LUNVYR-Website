Core Materials

Material Portfolio

Natural Brass

Natural Brass

Premium brass alloy that develops rich patina over time, creating a living finish that evolves with your space.

  • 60% copper, 40% zinc composition
  • Develops natural patina over time
  • Excellent corrosion resistance
  • Warm golden undertones
  • Hand-polishable to high shine
Aged Brass

Aged Brass

Pre-patinated brass with accelerated aging process, providing instant vintage character and depth.

  • Chemical patina application
  • Rich brown and green tones
  • Sealed protective coating
  • Consistent aged appearance
  • Low maintenance finish
Blackened Steel

Blackened Steel

Heat-treated steel with dark oxide finish, offering industrial aesthetic with superior durability.

  • High-carbon steel base
  • Heat-oxidized surface treatment
  • Matte black appearance
  • Enhanced corrosion protection
  • Industrial aesthetic appeal
Polished Steel

Polished Steel

Mirror-finished stainless steel providing contemporary elegance with superior corrosion resistance.

  • 316L marine-grade stainless
  • Mirror polish finish
  • Excellent corrosion resistance
  • Modern aesthetic appeal
  • Easy maintenance and cleaning

Finish Techniques

Finish Processes

Hand Polishing Process

Our master craftsmen use traditional hand-polishing techniques to achieve mirror-like finishes on brass and steel surfaces.

1

Surface Preparation

Remove oxidation and surface imperfections with progressively finer abrasives.

2

Compound Application

Apply specialized polishing compounds using buffing wheels and hand techniques.

3

Final Buffing

Achieve mirror finish through progressive buffing with soft cotton wheels.

4

Protection

Apply clear protective coating to preserve shine and slow natural patina development.

Chemical Patina Development

Accelerated aging using chemical solutions creates rich, complex patina finishes with consistent coloration.

1

Surface Cleaning

Thorough degreasing and cleaning to ensure uniform patina adhesion.

2

Chemical Application

Apply patina solutions in controlled conditions for even color development.

3

Color Development

Monitor patina progression and build layers for desired depth and hue.

4

Sealing

Apply protective wax or lacquer to stabilize the patina and prevent further aging.

Heat Blackening Treatment

High-temperature oxidation creates durable black finishes on steel with enhanced corrosion resistance.

1

Heat Treatment

Controlled heating in oxidizing atmosphere forms iron oxide layer.

2

Oxide Formation

Monitor temperature and timing for optimal oxide thickness and adhesion.

3

Cooling

Controlled cooling process stabilizes the oxide layer and color.

4

Final Treatment

Apply penetrating oil or wax for additional protection and depth.

Maintenance

Care Guidelines

Daily Cleaning

  • Dust with microfiber cloth
  • Use warm water for light cleaning
  • Dry immediately after cleaning
  • Avoid harsh chemicals

Periodic Maintenance

  • Polish brass monthly
  • Apply protective wax
  • Check for wear spots
  • Touch up finishes as needed

Problem Solving

  • Remove water spots with vinegar
  • Buff out minor scratches
  • Restore tarnished areas
  • Professional refinishing available

Best Practices

  • Use appropriate cleaning products
  • Follow manufacturer guidelines
  • Document maintenance schedule
  • Store care products safely
