Our Story

From sketch to quiet drama

LUNVYR began with an obsession for the dialogue between light and material. Each piece starts as a graphite study, moves into scale models, and is refined through countless iterations until the form feels inevitable.

We work slowly: metal carefully shaped and finished, brass detailed and patinated, optics tuned to achieve a calm, architectural glow. The result is lighting that feels collected rather than manufactured.

Our studio collaborates with architects and interior designers to tailor proportion, finish and mounting—delivering fixtures that integrate seamlessly into the built environment.
