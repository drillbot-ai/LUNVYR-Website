Our Story

Where Light Meets Art

LUNVYR was born from a passion for transforming spaces through exceptional lighting. Founded in New York, our atelier combines traditional metalworking techniques with contemporary design sensibilities to create fixtures that are both functional art and architectural elements.

"Every piece tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship, where each curve, finish, and light distribution is carefully considered to create an experience, not just illumination."

Our collections—from the geometric elegance of AUREA to the industrial strength of BLACK LABEL—reflect our commitment to diverse aesthetic languages while maintaining our signature attention to detail and quality.

Working closely with architects, interior designers, and discerning homeowners, we create lighting solutions that enhance rather than compete with their environments, becoming integral parts of the architectural narrative.

Our Philosophy

The principles that guide every design decision and craftsmanship detail.

Timeless Design

We create fixtures that transcend trends, focusing on proportions and forms that will remain elegant for decades. Each design is refined until it achieves that rare quality of feeling both contemporary and eternal.

Artisanal Excellence

Every piece is handcrafted in our New York atelier using traditional metalworking techniques combined with modern precision. Our artisans bring decades of experience to each fixture, ensuring uncompromising quality.

Architectural Integration

Lighting should enhance, not dominate. Our fixtures are designed to complement architectural spaces, creating atmosphere and highlighting the beauty of the environments they illuminate.

Sustainable Craft

We believe in creating pieces built to last generations. Using premium materials, repairable construction, and timeless design, our fixtures represent an investment in sustainable luxury.

Custom Solutions

No two spaces are identical. We work closely with clients to adapt our designs or create entirely bespoke pieces that perfectly complement their architectural vision and functional requirements.

Material Honesty

We celebrate the inherent beauty of our materials—brass develops its natural patina, metals age gracefully, and finishes are applied to enhance rather than disguise the underlying material qualities.

Process

01 — Concept & Design

Every piece begins with extensive research into architectural trends, material properties, and lighting physics. Our designers create detailed sketches and digital models to perfect proportions and form.

02 — Prototyping & Testing

We create physical prototypes to test light distribution, material behavior, and structural integrity. Each prototype undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

03 — Artisanal Crafting

Master craftsmen shape, weld, and finish each component by hand in our New York atelier. Traditional metalworking techniques are combined with modern precision tools for exceptional quality.

04 — Finishing & Assembly

Surfaces are meticulously prepared and finished with our signature patinas and protective coatings. Electrical components are installed and tested to UL standards before final quality inspection.

05 — Quality Assurance

Each finished piece undergoes comprehensive testing including electrical safety, structural integrity, and finish quality. Only fixtures that meet our exacting standards bear the LUNVYR name.
